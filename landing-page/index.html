The Problem: Brand Protection is Inaccessible

Enterprise brands spend millions on legal teams to police the web. SMEs are left to Google themselves and hope for the best. When someone misuses your brand online, it directly impacts your bottom line.

Customers Diverted

Over 2.3 million typosquatting domains have been registered to mimic popular brands, sending buyers to competitors or scam sites.

Record Domain Disputes

WIPO handled a record 6,168 domain disputes in 2024. Retail (37%) and finance (13%) are the most targeted sectors.

Clone Apps Trade on Trust

Nearly 97,000 apps were delisted from Google Play in Feb 2025 alone, bypassing store security entirely.

The Solution

We continuously monitor the web for potential infringements—and use an LLM via OpenRouter to separate genuine threats from the noise.

1

Set up your brand profile

Enter brand names, related keywords, and official domains to configure the monitoring scope.

2

Apify Actors scan the web

A suite of official and custom Apify actors continuously monitors social media, search results, domain registries, and app stores.

3

AI analyses every finding

An LLM classifies intent and scores severity, filtering out false positives (e.g. distinguishing a fan page from an impersonation).

4

Ranked Digest

Findings are prioritized automatically, accompanied by plain-language explanations of the concrete business risk.

Core AI Value

Classifies Intent

Goes beyond dumb keyword matching to understand context at scale—separating legitimate mentions from malicious activity.

Scores Severity

Doesn't just dump raw data. Automatically prioritizes threats so users can act immediately on what matters most.

Plain English Context

Translates complex data points into a human-readable summary of the finding, the flag reason, and the risk.
