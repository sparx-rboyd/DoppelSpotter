The Problem: Brand Protection is Inaccessible

Enterprise brands spend millions on legal teams to police the web. SMEs are left to Google themselves and hope for the best. When someone misuses your brand online, it directly impacts your bottom line.

Customers Diverted

Over 2.3 million typosquatting domains have been registered to mimic popular brands, sending buyers to competitors or scam sites.

Record Domain Disputes

WIPO handled a record 6,168 domain disputes in 2024. Retail (37%) and finance (13%) are the most targeted sectors.

Clone Apps Trade on Trust

Nearly 97,000 apps were delisted from Google Play in Feb 2025 alone, bypassing store security entirely.

The Solution

We continually monitor the web for potential infringements—and use an LLM via OpenRouter to separate genuine threats from the noise.

1

Set up your brand profile

Enter brand names, related keywords, and official domains to configure the monitoring scope.

2

Apify Actors scan the web

A suite of official and custom Apify actors continually monitors social media, search results, domain registries, and code repositories.

3

AI analyses every finding

An LLM classifies intent and scores severity, filtering out false positives (e.g., distinguishing a fan page from an impersonation).

4

Ranked Digest

Findings are prioritised automatically, accompanied by plain-English explanations of the concrete business risk.

Core AI Value

Classifies Intent

Goes beyond dumb keyword matching to understand context at scale—separating legitimate mentions from malicious activity.

Scores Severity

Doesn't just dump raw data. Automatically prioritises threats so users can act immediately on what matters most.

Plain English Context

Translates complex data points into a human-readable summary of the finding, the flag reason, and the risk.

Built with Generative & Agentic AI

AI isn't just a feature in DoppelSpotter; it's the foundation of how it was built and how it operates.

Agentic AI Development

We utilised Cursor's agentic AI for end-to-end development. From writing this pitch and building the landing page, to making core architectural decisions, to designing, building, testing, and deploying both the Next.js app and our custom Apify actor.

Generative Analysis Engine

The core of our app's pipeline uses claude-3.5-haiku via OpenRouter. It performs complex intent classification, severity scoring, and generates plain-English, scan-level summaries of all findings—separating real threats from the noise.

AI-Enriched Actor

Our custom published actor, recent-domain-registrations, isn't a simple API wrapper. It intrinsically uses Generative AI during the scraping process to perform enhanced homepage analysis, actively assessing the visual and textual content of new domains for phishing intent.
