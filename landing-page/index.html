The Problem: Brand Protection is Inaccessible

Enterprise brands spend millions on legal teams to police the web. SMEs are left to Google themselves and hope for the best. When someone misuses your brand online, it directly impacts your bottom line.

Customers Diverted

Over 2.3 million typosquatting domains have been registered to mimic popular brands, sending buyers to competitors or scam sites.

Record Domain Disputes

WIPO handled a record 6,168 domain disputes in 2024. Retail (37%) and finance (13%) are the most targeted sectors.

Clone Apps Trade on Trust

Nearly 97,000 apps were delisted from Google Play in Feb 2025 alone, bypassing store security entirely.

The Solution

We continually monitor the web for potential infringements using DeepSeek 3.2 via OpenRouter to separate genuine threats from the noise, then generate succinct analyses for each finding.

1

Set up your brand profile

Enter your brand name, related keywords, and official domains to set the monitoring scope.

2

Apify Actors scan the web

A suite of official and community actors, plus two custom actors we built and published (domain registrations and EUIPO trademarks), monitor web results, social platforms, domain registries, trademark filings, app stores and code repositories.

3

AI analyses every finding

DeepSeek 3.2 classifies intent and scores severity, filtering out false positives (e.g. distinguishing a fan page from an impersonation).

4

Ranked Digest

Findings are prioritised automatically, accompanied by plain-English explanations of the concrete business risk.

5

Take Action

Rich, visual insights, downloadable PDF and CSV reports, and finding management tools support rapid follow-on action for high risk threats.

Core AI Value

Intent Classification

Goes beyond dumb keyword matching to understand context at scale - separating legitimate mentions from malicious activity.

Severity Scoring

Doesn't just dump raw data. Automatically prioritises threats so users can act immediately on what matters most.

Plain English Context

Translates complex data points into a human-readable summary of the finding, the reason for flagging, and the potential risk.

Built with Generative & Agentic AI

AI isn't just a feature in DoppelSpotter; it's the foundation of how it was built and how it operates.

Agentic AI Development

We utilised Cursor's agentic AI for end-to-end development. From writing this pitch, to making core architectural decisions, to designing, building, testing, and deploying the Next.js app and both of our custom Apify actors (domain registrations and EUIPO trademarks).

Generative Analysis Engine

The core of our app's pipeline uses deepseek-3.2 via OpenRouter. It performs complex intent classification, severity scoring, and generates plain-English, scan-level summaries of all findings - separating real threats from the noise.

Custom-Built Actors

We published two custom actors to the Apify Store. recent-domain-registrations uses Generative AI internally to visit and analyse new domains for potential domain squatting`. euipo-trademark-search queries the EU trademark registry with wildcard RSQL and cached OAuth2 token management.
